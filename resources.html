Reviews

  • G. Carleo et al., "Machine learning and the physical sciences", Rev. Mod. Phys. 91 045002 (2019), doi:10.1103/RevModPhys.91.045002, arXiv:1903.10563.
  • D. Guest et al., "Deep Learning and its application to LHC physics", Annu. Rev. Nucl. Part. Sci. 68, 161 (2018), doi:10.1146/annurev-nucl-101917-021019, arXiv:1806.11484.
